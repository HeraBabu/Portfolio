Here is my story

I’m a tech-enthusiastic career changer with an academic background in Bioinformatics and growing experience in UX design and web development.

I’m especially passionate about the intersection between technology and people – whether that’s optimizing digital workflows, improving usability, or working on agile software projects.

Quick Facts

  • Currently Living In:
    Munich, Germany
    Living in Germany for over 10 years
  • Hometown:
    The Nilgiris, India
  • First Encounter With Programming:
    In 2000, as a curious 3rd grader, I typed my very first command in LOGO Programming — and watched a tiny triangle draw a square on the screen. That moment sparked a fascination with how simple code can create something visual, and I’ve been exploring ever since.
  • Education:
    - Bachelor’s in Bioinformatics, Karunya University, India
    - Master's in Bioinformatics (unfinished), Saarland University, Germany
    - Countless online tutorials, self-directed courses, and hands-on learning
  • What I Love Building:
    Intuitive user interfaces, design systems, and tools that make things simpler. I enjoy problem-solving through structure and clarity — and a touch of aesthetic obsession.
  • Languages:
    English (fluent), German (B2), Tamil (native), Kannada (conversational)
